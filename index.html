About

What is Spokes?

Spokes hub is for mountain bike riders. The purpose is to share their trips on a platform so that other riders can view and use these routes for them selves.

Whats our vision?

Our Vision is to build a community of people that have a common interest in the world of mountain biking.

Whats our aim?

Our aim, once the community is built, to post group rides and suggested routes that people have shown an interest in. So it encourages more people to join up.

Trail

Jane
Cube Hardtail
I have named this trail Windy Cliffs due to the location of the last section of the trail along the top of the purbecks near Studland. The beginning of this ride is quite scenic, leading from open heathland, into the woods and then traversing up and along the clifftops and then leading downhill to finish the loop. Be aware of the top section as it can get quite windy other than that enjoy the ride. It is around 45km distance
